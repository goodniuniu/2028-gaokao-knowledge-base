## 核心内容

> **使用说明**：按"主题词块（chunk）"而非单词记忆——读后续写段落的最小输出单位是搭配。每主题精选高频词块，背时连例句一起记。

### 一、成长 / 自我突破（Growth & Self-breakthrough）

| 词块 | 释义 | 用法提示 |
|------|------|----------|
| step out of one's comfort zone | 走出舒适区 | 成长类万能 |
| build up confidence | 建立自信 | confidence 不可数 |
| rise to the challenge | 迎难而上 | challenge 可数 |
| learn a lesson from... | 从……中吸取教训 | lesson 可数 |
| a sense of achievement | 成就感 | achievement 可数 |
| become a better version of oneself | 成为更好的自己 | 升华句 |
| overcome the inner fear | 克服内心恐惧 | overcome-overcame-overcome |

**示范句**：Stepping out of my comfort zone, I finally rose to the challenge and felt a real sense of achievement.

### 二、亲情 / 家庭（Family & Kinship）

| 词块 | 释义 | 用法提示 |
|------|------|----------|
| a warm hand on one's shoulder | 一只温暖的手搭在肩上 | 亲情动作 |
| a gentle smile of encouragement | 鼓励的温柔微笑 | encouragement 不可数 |
| feel the love of family | 感受家人的爱 | 情感升华 |
| be there for sb. through thick and thin | 患难与共陪伴某人 | thick and thin 固定 |
| bond between parents and children | 亲子纽带 | bond 可数 |

**示范句**：Feeling the love of family, I realized that a gentle smile of encouragement could heal more than words.

### 三、友情 / 互助（Friendship & Mutual Help）

| 词块 | 释义 | 用法提示 |
|------|------|----------|
| lend a helping hand | 伸出援手 | 助人场景 |
| a true friend in need | 患难真友 | 谚语变体 |
| stand by one's side | 站在某人身旁支持 | stand by 不背 |
| share weal and woe | 同甘共苦 | weal=幸福 |
| strengthen the bond of friendship | 增进友谊纽带 | strengthen 动词 |

**示范句**：He lent a helping hand without hesitation, and from that day our bond of friendship grew stronger.

### 四、助人 / 善意 / 信守承诺（Kindness & Keeping Promise）

| 词块 | 释义 | 用法提示 |
|------|------|----------|
| a small act of kindness | 一个小善举 | kindness 不可数 |
| keep one's promise / word | 信守诺言 | keep-kept-kept |
| a stranger's trust | 陌生人的信任 | trust 不可数 |
| repay one's kindness | 回报善意 | repay-repaid |
| be worth more than money | 比金钱更珍贵 | 品德升华 |

**示范句**：It dawned on me that a stranger's trust was worth more than money, and I was determined to keep my promise.

### 五、自然 / 环境烘托（Nature as Mood）

| 词块 | 释义 | 用法提示 |
|------|------|----------|
| a cold, wet evening | 寒冷潮湿的夜晚 | 2024真题原文 |
| a ray of sunlight broke through the clouds | 一缕阳光穿透云层 | 希望隐喻 |
| the gentle breeze | 微风 | breeze 可数 |
| under the starry sky | 星空下 | starry 形容词 |

**示范句**：Under the starry sky, a sense of peace washed over me and my anxiety melted away.

### 六、主题词汇学习法

| 方法 | 操作 | 频次 |
|------|------|------|
| 主题打包背 | 按主题整体背词块，不按字母表 | 每周1主题 |
| 三秒反应 | 看中文说英文词块，3秒内 | 每天10个 |
| 段落输出 | 每主题用 4-5 词块写 80 词续写片段 | 每主题1段 |
