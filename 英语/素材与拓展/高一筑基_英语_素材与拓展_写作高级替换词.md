---

## 核心内容

> **使用原则**：①**先准确，后高级**——搭配拿不准就用简单词；②**一文多换**——同一低级词在全文出现3次以上是低档信号；③**分文体选词**——应用文要得体，读后续写要生动。

### 一、形容词升级

| 常用词 | 高级替换 | 使用场景 | 例句 |
|--------|----------|----------|------|
| important | **crucial / vital / significant / essential** | 应用文论述、读后续写点题 | Practice is **essential** to improving spoken English. |
| good | **excellent / remarkable / beneficial / fantastic** | 评价事物（应用文） | The lecture was **remarkable** and **beneficial** to us all. |
| bad | **awful / terrible / harmful / negative** | 描述问题 | Staying up late has a **negative** effect on our health. |
| many | **numerous / a large number of / countless** | 强调数量 | **Numerous** students took part in the activity. |
| big | **enormous / huge / vast** | 形容规模、变化 | The project requires an **enormous** amount of effort. |
| happy | **delighted / thrilled / overjoyed** | 读后续写情感描写 | I was **thrilled** to receive the award. |
| sad | **sorrowful / heartbroken / downcast** | 读后续写情感低谷 | She was **heartbroken** at the news. |
| afraid | **frightened / terrified / scared to death** | 读后续写紧张恐惧 | **Terrified**, he froze where he stood. |
| beautiful | **breathtaking / gorgeous / fascinating** | 景物描写 | The view from the top was simply **breathtaking**. |
| difficult | **challenging / demanding** | 委婉说"难" | The task is **challenging** but rewarding. |
| interesting | **appealing / fascinating / attractive** | 推荐类应用文 | The club offers many **appealing** activities. |
| helpful | **beneficial / rewarding / of great help** | 活动评价 | The volunteer work proved highly **rewarding**. |

### 二、动词升级

| 常用词 | 高级替换 | 搭配/文体提示 | 例句 |
|--------|----------|---------------|------|
| think | **hold the view that / be convinced that / figure** | 表达观点（应用文） | I **am convinced that** practice makes perfect. |
| want | **be eager to / long to / desire to** | 表达愿望 | We **long to** explore the unknown world. |
| like | **be fond of / be fascinated by / have a passion for** | 兴趣爱好 | I **have a passion for** traditional Chinese painting. |
| know | **be aware of / realize / come to understand** | 认识类 | Gradually I **came to understand** his kindness. |
| get | **obtain / acquire / gain** | 获取知识技能 | Reading helps us **acquire** knowledge and **gain** wisdom. |
| try | **make every effort to / spare no effort to / attempt to** | 努力做 | We should **spare no effort to** protect the environment. |
| help | **assist / give sb a hand / be of help** | 应用文求助/帮助 | I would appreciate it if you could **assist** me. |
| use | **make use of / take advantage of / apply** | 利用 | **Make full use of** every chance to practice English. |
| improve | **enhance / strengthen / polish** | 提升能力 | The course greatly **enhanced** my writing skills. |
| remember | **keep in mind / bear in mind that** | 叮嘱（应用文） | Please **bear in mind that** safety comes first. |
| cause | **lead to / result in / contribute to** | 因果论证 | Carelessness may **result in** serious consequences. |
| solve | **tackle / address / work out** | 解决问题 | Effective measures should be taken to **address** this problem. |
| finish | **accomplish / complete / fulfill** | 完成任务 | We **accomplished** the task ahead of schedule. |

### 三、副词/程度词升级

| 常用词 | 高级替换 | 使用场景 | 例句 |
|--------|----------|----------|------|
| very | **extremely / remarkably / incredibly / more than** | 万能程度升级 | The performance was **incredibly** moving. |
| very (+adj.) | **more than + adj.**（非常） | 含蓄高级 | I am **more than** delighted to receive your invitation. |
| suddenly | **all of a sudden / all at once / out of nowhere** | 读后续写情节突转 | **All of a sudden**, the lights went out. |
| finally | **eventually / ultimately / in the end** | 叙事结尾 | **Eventually**, our efforts paid off. |
| immediately | **without delay / without hesitation / in no time** | 紧急动作 | He rushed to help **without hesitation**. |
| really | **genuinely / truly / sincerely** | 强调真实 | I **genuinely** appreciate your help. |
| luckily | **fortunately / to one's relief** | 读后续写转危为安 | **To our relief**, the lost dog was found safe. |
| unluckily | **unfortunately / to one's disappointment** | 转折失落 | **To my disappointment**, the match was cancelled. |
| almost | **nearly / virtually / just about** | — | The stadium was **virtually** empty. |

### 四、连接词升级（应用文逻辑骨架）

| 功能 | 低级 | 高级替换 | 文体提示 |
|------|------|----------|----------|
| 首先 | first | **to begin with / first of all / first and foremost** | 通用 |
| 其次 | second | **what's more / moreover / furthermore / in addition** | 应用文递进 |
| 最后 | finally | **last but not least / ultimately** | 应用文收尾 |
| 但是 | but | **however / nevertheless / yet** | 句首用however+, |
| 所以 | so | **therefore / thus / as a result / consequently** | 因果论证 |
| 因为 | because | **since / as / now that / due to / owing to** | due to/owing to接名词 |
| 总之 | in a word | **in conclusion / to sum up / all in all / on the whole** | 结尾段 |
| 事实上 | in fact | **as a matter of fact / actually / in reality** | — |
| 例如 | for example | **for instance / take...for example / a case in point** | 例证 |
| 一方面……另一方面 | on one hand... | **for one thing...for another...** | 并列论证 |

### 五、应用文专用高频升级句块

| 低级表达 | 高级表达 |
|----------|----------|
| I'm writing to tell you... | I'm writing to **extend my sincere invitation / express my heartfelt gratitude**... |
| I hope you can come. | I would **appreciate** it if you could **spare the time** to come. |
| Thank you very much. | Words fail to **convey** my gratitude. / I'm **deeply grateful** for... |
| I'm sorry for... | Please accept my **sincere apologies** for... |
| I want to ask for help. | I'm writing to **ask a favor of you**. / I wonder if you could **do me a favor**. |
| Looking forward to your reply. | Your early reply will be **highly appreciated**. |

### 六、读后续写专用情感升级

| 低级 | 高级 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| very happy | **joy welled up inside sb / sb's face lit up** | Her face **lit up** at the sight of the gift. |
| very sad | **tears streamed down one's cheeks / be overwhelmed with sorrow** | Tears **streaming down** his cheeks, he hugged his mother tightly. |
| very nervous | **one's heart raced / palms sweating / butterflies in one's stomach** | My heart **raced** as I stepped onto the stage. |
| said angrily | **snapped / roared / yelled** | "That's enough!" he **snapped**. |
| walked slowly | **dragged one's feet / made one's way with difficulty** | Exhausted, she **dragged her feet** toward home. |

### 七、使用避坑指南

| 坑 | 说明 | 正例 |
|----|------|------|
| 高级词堆砌 | 一段5个生僻词反而显得做作，每段1-2处升级即可 | 升级关键动词和情感词，连接词保持清晰 |
| 搭配错误 | crucial for/of混用、be aware后接to do | be aware **of** sth；be crucial **to/for** sth |
| 文体错位 | 应用文中用snapped、terrified等口语化强烈词 | 应用文选正式词（appreciate/sincere），读后续写选生动词 |
| 拼写冒险 | 考场拼不出的高级词不用 | beneficial拼不准就用helpful——写错的vital不如写对的important |

### 八、训练计划

| 周次 | 任务 |
|------|------|
| 第1周 | 背形容词+动词两类，每类自测汉译英 |
| 第2周 | 背副词+连接词，用高级连接词重写一段旧作文 |
| 第3周起 | 每写一篇作文，强制完成"3处替换"：找3个低级词换成高级词后再誊写 |

## 备注

- 写作评分中"词汇丰富性"的关键信号是**动词的生动性**和**情感词的准确性**——优先升级这两类，性价比最高。
- 建议建立个人"替换本"：把自己作文里出现过3次以上的低级词列出来，逐个配1个有把握的高级替换，宁少勿滥。
- 近5年新高考Ⅰ卷应用文情境偏校园与日常（建议、邀请、感谢），应用文专用句块（第五部分）可整句套用，背诵优先级最高。
- 考场铁律：**没默写对过的高级词，不要第一次用在高考卷上**。本卡所有替换词需在平时作文中至少成功使用2次方可列入考场清单。
