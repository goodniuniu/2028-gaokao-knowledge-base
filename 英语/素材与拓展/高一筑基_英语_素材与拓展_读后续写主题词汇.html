读后续写主题词汇

⚠️待强化★★☆☆☆#高一筑基#新高考Ⅰ卷·广东来源：新高考Ⅰ卷读后续写真题主题归纳（2021-2024）+ 课标主题语境分类
广东考情：高频（新高考Ⅰ卷读后续写25分）· 压轴 · 广东卷读后续写主题集中在"人与自我（成长/品德）""人与社会（助人/亲情/友情）"。本题库按主题打包核心词块，解决"写到相关情节却想不出词"的问题。难度定位基础：单个词不难，难在主题词块成组输出。

核心内容

使用说明：按"主题词块（chunk）"而非单词记忆——读后续写段落的最小输出单位是搭配。每主题精选高频词块，背时连例句一起记。

一、成长 / 自我突破（Growth & Self-breakthrough）

词块释义用法提示
step out of one's comfort zone走出舒适区成长类万能
build up confidence建立自信confidence 不可数
rise to the challenge迎难而上challenge 可数
learn a lesson from...从……中吸取教训lesson 可数
a sense of achievement成就感achievement 可数
become a better version of oneself成为更好的自己升华句
overcome the inner fear克服内心恐惧overcome-overcame-overcome

示范句：Stepping out of my comfort zone, I finally rose to the challenge and felt a real sense of achievement.

二、亲情 / 家庭（Family & Kinship）

词块释义用法提示
a warm hand on one's shoulder一只温暖的手搭在肩上亲情动作
a gentle smile of encouragement鼓励的温柔微笑encouragement 不可数
feel the love of family感受家人的爱情感升华
be there for sb. through thick and thin患难与共陪伴某人thick and thin 固定
bond between parents and children亲子纽带bond 可数

示范句：Feeling the love of family, I realized that a gentle smile of encouragement could heal more than words.

三、友情 / 互助（Friendship & Mutual Help）

词块释义用法提示
lend a helping hand伸出援手助人场景
a true friend in need患难真友谚语变体
stand by one's side站在某人身旁支持stand by 不背
share weal and woe同甘共苦weal=幸福
strengthen the bond of friendship增进友谊纽带strengthen 动词

示范句：He lent a helping hand without hesitation, and from that day our bond of friendship grew stronger.

四、助人 / 善意 / 信守承诺（Kindness & Keeping Promise）

词块释义用法提示
a small act of kindness一个小善举kindness 不可数
keep one's promise / word信守诺言keep-kept-kept
a stranger's trust陌生人的信任trust 不可数
repay one's kindness回报善意repay-repaid
be worth more than money比金钱更珍贵品德升华

示范句：It dawned on me that a stranger's trust was worth more than money, and I was determined to keep my promise.

五、自然 / 环境烘托（Nature as Mood）

词块释义用法提示
a cold, wet evening寒冷潮湿的夜晚2024真题原文
a ray of sunlight broke through the clouds一缕阳光穿透云层希望隐喻
the gentle breeze微风breeze 可数
under the starry sky星空下starry 形容词

示范句：Under the starry sky, a sense of peace washed over me and my anxiety melted away.
