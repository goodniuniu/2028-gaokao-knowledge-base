## 核心内容

> **使用说明**：词汇按"词块（chunk）"而非单词收录——写作和口语输出的最小单位是搭配，不是单词。每话题精选词块，背时连例句一起记，目标：**看到话题能脱口而出3-5个词块**。

### 一、环保（Environmental Protection）

| 词块 | 释义 | 用法提示 |
|------|------|----------|
| environmental protection | 环境保护 | 话题总起词 |
| raise people's awareness of... | 提高人们的……意识 | 写作万能动作 |
| take effective measures to do | 采取有效措施做某事 | 措施类句首 |
| save energy / conserve water | 节约能源/用水 | conserve比save正式 |
| reduce carbon emissions | 减少碳排放 | carbon emission可数 |
| sort the garbage / waste sorting | 垃圾分类 | 广东校园高频话题 |
| recyclable materials | 可回收材料 | re-前缀="再" |
| renewable energy | 可再生能源 | 如solar/wind power |
| white pollution / plastic waste | 白色污染/塑料垃圾 | 限塑话题 |
| protect wildlife / endangered species | 保护野生动物/濒危物种 | endangered=濒临灭绝的 |
| live in harmony with nature | 与自然和谐共处 | 升华句专用 |
| It's everyone's duty to protect the environment. | 保护环境人人有责 | 结尾呼吁句 |
| ban single-use plastics | 禁用一次性塑料 | ban sb/sth from doing |
| go green / live a low-carbon life | 绿色生活/低碳生活 | 口语写作两用 |
| pollute the air / air pollution | 污染空气/空气污染 | 动词名词都要会 |
| make a difference to the environment | 对环境产生影响 | make a difference万能词块 |

**写作示范句**：Only by working together can we live in harmony with nature and make a real difference to our planet.（倒装升华句）

### 二、科技（Science & Technology）

| 词块 | 释义 | 用法提示 |
|------|------|----------|
| artificial intelligence (AI) | 人工智能 | 近年阅读高频词 |
| with the development of technology | 随着科技的发展 | 开头万能句 |
| bring great convenience to our life | 给生活带来极大便利 | convenience不可数 |
| play an increasingly important role in... | 在……中发挥日益重要的作用 | increasingly=越来越 |
| change the way we live and work | 改变我们生活和工作的方式 | 定语从句活用 |
| rely too much on smart phones | 过度依赖智能手机 | 利弊类转折用 |
| be addicted to the Internet | 沉迷网络 | addicted to+名词/doing |
| online learning / distance education | 在线学习/远程教育 | 疫情后高频 |
| information technology | 信息技术 | 缩略IT |
| a double-edged sword | 双刃剑 | 利弊论证必备 |
| make full use of technology | 充分利用科技 | make use of家族 |
| scientific research | 科学研究 | research不可数 |
| technological innovation | 技术创新 | innovation名词 |
| keep pace with the times | 与时俱进 | 高级表达 |
| broaden our horizons | 开阔眼界 | 科技/教育/阅读话题通用 |
| improve efficiency | 提高效率 | efficient形容词 |

**写作示范句**：As a double-edged sword, AI brings great convenience to our life, but we should never rely too much on it.

### 三、教育（Education & Campus Life）

| 词块 | 释义 | 用法提示 |
|------|------|----------|
| receive a good education | 接受良好教育 | education前可用a |
| acquire knowledge / skills | 获取知识/技能 | acquire比get正式 |
| develop a good habit of reading | 养成阅读习惯 | develop/form a habit |
| arouse students' interest in learning | 激发学习兴趣 | arouse interest/curiosity |
| all-round development | 全面发展 | 德育体美劳话题 |
| reduce the burden on students | 减轻学生负担 | "双减"话题 |
| extracurricular activities | 课外活动 | 应用文高频 |
| learn through practice | 在实践中学习 | 实践类话题 |
| lay a solid foundation for... | 为……打下坚实基础 | foundation名词 |
| be admitted to a university | 被大学录取 | 被动语态 |
| face great pressure / relieve stress | 面对压力/缓解压力 | relieve=缓解 |
| build up confidence | 建立自信 | confidence不可数 |
| a harmonious campus | 和谐校园 | harmonious形容词 |
| take an active part in class | 积极参与课堂 | part前可加active |
| form a correct attitude towards learning | 树立正确的学习态度 | attitude towards/to |
| combine theory with practice | 理论联系实际 | combine A with B |

**写作示范句**：Only when we combine theory with practice can we lay a solid foundation for our future.

### 四、传统文化（Traditional Chinese Culture）

| 词块 | 释义 | 用法提示 |
|------|------|----------|
| traditional Chinese culture | 中国传统文化 | 话题总起词 |
| have a history of more than 5,000 years | 有五千多年历史 | 介绍文化万能句 |
| be deeply rooted in... | 深深植根于…… | 高级表达 |
| cultural heritage / relics | 文化遗产/遗迹 | heritage不可数 |
| intangible cultural heritage | 非物质文化遗产 | 申遗话题 |
| carry forward / promote traditional culture | 弘扬传统文化 | carry forward=弘扬 |
| a symbol of good luck | 好运的象征 | 介绍剪纸/中国结 |
| date back to / date from | 追溯到 | 无被动无进行时 |
| be known/famous for its unique style | 以独特风格闻名 | be known for=因……闻名 |
| paper cutting / calligraphy / Peking Opera | 剪纸/书法/京剧 | 三大国粹常考 |
| the Spring Festival / dragon boat racing | 春节/赛龙舟 | 节日话题 |
| reflect the wisdom of our ancestors | 体现祖先的智慧 | wisdom不可数 |
| be popular with people of all ages | 深受各年龄段喜爱 | 介绍活动结尾句 |
| strengthen cultural confidence | 增强文化自信 | 升华句 |
| serve as a bridge between the past and the present | 充当连接古今的桥梁 | serve as=充当 |
| appeal to young people | 吸引年轻人 | appeal to=吸引 |

**写作示范句**：Dating back to the Tang Dynasty, paper cutting is deeply rooted in Chinese culture and reflects the wisdom of our ancestors.

### 五、健康（Health）

| 词块 | 释义 | 用法提示 |
|------|------|----------|
| keep fit / stay healthy | 保持健康 | 话题总起词 |
| physical and mental health | 身心健康 | mental=心理的 |
| form a healthy lifestyle | 养成健康的生活方式 | lifestyle可数 |
| take exercise regularly / work out | 经常锻炼 | work out口语 |
| keep a balanced diet | 保持均衡饮食 | balanced形容词 |
| stay up late / get enough sleep | 熬夜/保证睡眠 | 反面/正面搭配 |
| be beneficial to our health | 对健康有益 | =be good for（升级） |
| do harm to / be harmful to | 对……有害 | harm不可数 |
| build up a strong body | 强健体魄 | build up=增强 |
| relieve stress and anxiety | 缓解压力与焦虑 | anxiety名词 |
| develop a positive attitude | 培养积极心态 | positive attitude |
| a healthy diet rich in vegetables | 富含蔬菜的健康饮食 | rich in=富含 |
| as the saying goes, "Health is wealth" | 俗话说"健康即财富" | 引用句 |
| give up junk food | 戒掉垃圾食品 | give up+名词 |
| It is strongly recommended that... | 强烈建议…… | 建议信句型 |

**写作示范句**：As the saying goes, "Health is wealth." Only by keeping a balanced diet and taking exercise regularly can we build up a strong body.

### 六、话题词汇学习法

| 方法 | 操作 | 频次 |
|------|------|------|
| **话题打包背** | 按话题整体背词块，不按字母表背 | 每周1个话题 |
| **三秒反应训练** | 看中文说英文词块，3秒内说出 | 每天10个 |
| **造句输出** | 每个话题用5个词块写一段80词短文 | 每话题1篇 |
| **听说联动** | 把词块大声朗读并录音自听，服务Part A/B | 每周2次 |

## 备注

- 五大话题覆盖了近5年新高考Ⅰ卷写作与阅读的主要命题语境：2023新高考Ⅰ卷应用文（给外教提分组建议）、读后续写（写作比赛，成长话题）均可用本卡词块应对。
- 优先级建议：环保、科技、传统文化三话题近年热度最高，先背；教育、健康为保底话题。
- 词块记忆必须"英汉互译双向"：看到"弘扬传统文化"能写carry forward traditional culture，看到carry forward能反应中文——单向记忆在写作中会失灵。
- 广东听说Part B三问五答中，"健康生活""校园活动"类话题常见，词块朗读时注意连读与重音（如EXtracurricular重音在第三音节）。
