## 核心内容

> **使用原则**：听说词汇与笔试词汇标准不同——听说要求**发音准、反应快、敢开口**。每个词都要做到"听到能懂、想到能说、说出不错音"，而不是只认识拼写。

### 一、情感类词汇（Part C复述必考，Part B五答高频）

#### 1. 积极情感

| 词汇 | 音标提示 | 中文 | 例句（复述可用） |
|------|----------|------|------------------|
| happy | /ˈhæpi/ | 高兴的 | Tom was very happy to receive the gift. |
| excited | /ɪkˈsaɪtɪd/ | 兴奋的 | The children were excited about the trip. |
| grateful | /ˈɡreɪtfl/ | 感激的 | She was grateful to the stranger for his help. |
| thankful | /ˈθæŋkfl/ | 感谢的 | I am thankful for everything my parents have done. |
| proud | /praʊd/ | 自豪的 | His parents were proud of his progress. |
| satisfied | /ˈsætɪsfaɪd/ | 满意的 | The teacher was satisfied with our performance. |
| moved / touched | /muːvd/ /tʌtʃt/ | 感动的 | I was deeply moved by the story. |
| relaxed | /rɪˈlækst/ | 放松的 | After the exam, we all felt relaxed. |
| hopeful | /ˈhəʊpfl/ | 充满希望的 | We are hopeful about the future. |
| surprised | /səˈpraɪzd/ | 惊讶的 | To my surprise, he won first prize. |

#### 2. 消极情感

| 词汇 | 中文 | 例句（复述可用） |
|------|------|------------------|
| sad / upset | 难过的/沮丧的 | She felt upset when she lost her wallet. |
| disappointed | 失望的 | He was disappointed with the result. |
| worried | 担心的 | The mother was worried about her sick son. |
| nervous | 紧张的 | I felt nervous before the speech contest. |
| afraid / scared | 害怕的 | The little girl was afraid of the dark. |
| angry | 生气的 | He was angry because someone broke his model. |
| lonely | 孤独的 | The old man often felt lonely. |
| embarrassed | 尴尬的 | She felt embarrassed when she forgot the name. |
| regretful | 后悔的 | He was regretful for wasting so much time. |
| tired / exhausted | 疲惫的/精疲力竭的 | After the long walk, they were exhausted. |

#### 3. 情感转折表达（复述高潮必备）

| 表达 | 中文 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| To one's surprise | 令某人惊讶的是 | To his surprise, the door was open. |
| To one's joy / delight | 令某人高兴的是 | To her joy, she passed the exam. |
| To one's disappointment | 令某人失望的是 | To their disappointment, the game was cancelled. |
| Fortunately / Luckily | 幸运的是 | Luckily, a kind driver stopped to help. |
| Unfortunately / Unluckily | 不幸的是 | Unfortunately, it began to rain heavily. |
| Suddenly / All of a sudden | 突然 | All of a sudden, the lights went out. |

> **记忆口诀**：**"情绪词配故事线，先抑后扬最常见；转折副词放句首，复述立马有层次。"**

### 二、动作类动词（Part C复述骨架，笔记速记核心）

> 故事复述=动作链条。以下按故事发展顺序整理高频动词短语。

#### 1. 起因类动作

| 动词短语 | 中文 | 例句 |
|----------|------|------|
| decide to do | 决定做 | He decided to raise money for the sick boy. |
| plan to do | 计划做 | They planned to hold a surprise party. |
| want to do / would like to do | 想要做 | She wanted to buy a gift for her mother. |
| forget to do | 忘记做 | Tom forgot to bring his umbrella. |
| lose / miss | 丢失/错过 | He lost his wallet on the way home. |
| break / drop | 打破/掉落 | The boy dropped the vase and it broke. |

#### 2. 发展类动作

| 动词短语 | 中文 | 例句 |
|----------|------|------|
| try to do | 努力做 | She tried to fix the bike by herself. |
| ask sb. for help | 向某人求助 | He asked the policeman for help. |
| offer to do | 主动提出做 | A stranger offered to give him a lift. |
| help sb. (to) do | 帮助某人做 | The manager helped him choose a gift. |
| look for / search for | 寻找 | They looked for the lost dog everywhere. |
| find / discover | 找到/发现 | Finally, they found the dog in the park. |
| pick up | 捡起/接人 | She picked up the wallet and returned it. |
| give back / return | 归还 | The boy returned the money to the old man. |

#### 3. 结局类动作

| 动词短语 | 中文 | 例句 |
|----------|------|------|
| succeed in doing | 成功做 | He succeeded in passing the driving test. |
| fail to do | 未能做 | They failed to catch the last bus. |
| manage to do | 设法做成 | She managed to finish the work on time. |
| realize | 意识到 | He realized that honesty was important. |
| learn a lesson | 吸取教训 | From then on, he learned a lesson. |
| thank sb. for sth. | 因某事感谢 | She thanked the doctor for saving her dog. |
| apologize to sb. | 向某人道歉 | He apologized to his friend for being late. |
| become friends | 成为朋友 | The two boys became good friends. |

> **使用提示**：Part C笔记第一遍只听"动作链"（decide → ask → help → find → thank），细节第二遍补。动作动词是复述的骨架，骨架在故事就在。

### 三、场景类词汇（Part B情景对话+Part C故事背景）

#### 1. 校园场景（最高频）

| 分类 | 词汇与短语 | 例句 |
|------|------------|------|
| 课程学习 | subject, exam, test, homework, review, prepare for | I have to prepare for the English exam. |
| 校园活动 | sports meeting, speech contest, art festival, club, volunteer | Our school will hold an art festival next week. |
| 人物角色 | headmaster, classmate, roommate, monitor, librarian | The monitor organized a class meeting. |
| 地点设施 | classroom, library, playground, lab, canteen, dormitory | Let's meet at the school library. |
| 常用表达 | hand in, put off, take part in, sign up for, make progress | I want to sign up for the speech contest. |

#### 2. 家庭场景

| 分类 | 词汇与短语 | 例句 |
|------|------------|------|
| 家庭成员 | parents, grandparents, elder sister, cousin, uncle, aunt | My grandparents live in the countryside. |
| 家务活动 | do housework, cook dinner, wash dishes, clean the room | I often help my mother do housework. |
| 亲情时刻 | birthday, surprise, gift, celebrate, family dinner | We had a big family dinner on her birthday. |
| 常用表达 | take care of, look after, be proud of, be strict with | My parents are strict with me, but they love me. |

#### 3. 购物场景

| 分类 | 词汇与短语 | 例句 |
|------|------------|------|
| 场所物品 | shop, supermarket, mall, market, gift, toy, book | I bought a gift in the supermarket. |
| 价格支付 | price, cost, cheap, expensive, discount, pay for | The bag costs 50 yuan. / It is on discount. |
| 交易表达 | Can I help you? / How much is it? / I'll take it. | —How much is the ticket? —It's 30 yuan. |
| 常用表达 | can't afford, save money, spend...on, be worth | He saved money to buy a gift for his mother. |

#### 4. 旅行场景

| 分类 | 词汇与短语 | 例句 |
|------|------------|------|
| 出行方式 | by bus / train / plane / bike, on foot, take a taxi | We went there by train. |
| 旅行活动 | visit, tour, sightseeing, take photos, climb, camp | We took many photos during the trip. |
| 住宿票务 | hotel, book a room, ticket, check in, luggage | I booked a room online in advance. |
| 常用表达 | set off, arrive at, get lost, have a good time | We set off early in the morning. |

|------------|------|------|

### 四、复述万能过渡句（Part C保命句库）

> 卡壳时、串联时、开头结尾时的"万能胶水句"，背熟即可让复述流畅成型。

#### 1. 开头句

| 句型 | 适用 |
|------|------|
| One day, ... | 通用开头 |
| Last week / It was a sunny morning, ... | 有时间信息时 |
| The story is about ... | 概括式开头（保险） |
| ... was a kind / hard-working boy / girl. | 介绍人物开头 |

#### 2. 发展串联句

| 句型 | 适用 |
|------|------|
| Then, ... / After that, ... | 顺承 |
| But / However, ... | 转折（故事必有转折） |
| Suddenly / At that moment, ... | 突发 |
| So / As a result, ... | 因果 |
| To his / her surprise, ... | 情感转折 |

#### 3. 高潮句

| 句型 | 适用 |
|------|------|
| Just when he was about to give up, ... | 坚持类故事 |
| What moved him most was that ... | 感动类故事 |
| Luckily, someone came to help. | 遇困获助类 |
| He tried again and again, and finally ... | 努力成功类 |

#### 4. 结尾句

| 句型 | 适用 |
|------|------|
| Finally / In the end, ... | 通用结局 |
| From then on, ... | 感悟改变类 |
| He learned that ... / He realized that ... | 点题升华 |
| They all lived a happy life. / They became good friends. | 圆满结局 |
| What a meaningful day it was! | 感叹收尾 |

#### 5. 卡壳缓冲句（流利度救命）

| 句型 | 用法 |
|------|------|
| Let me see... / Well, ... | 争取1-2秒思考时间 |
| And then... / What's more, ... | 顺接下一句 |
| In other words, ... | 换个说法重述 |
| That is to say, ... | 解释补充 |

> **原则**：缓冲句只救急，一篇复述最多用1-2次，用多了反而显得不流利。

### 五、听说词汇记忆法

| 方法 | 操作 | 说明 |
|------|------|------|
| **场景串联法** | 按"故事线"记词：decide → try → fail → ask for help → succeed → be grateful | 词汇串成故事，复述时自然带出 |
| **听音辨形法** | 听单词录音，不看拼写复述发音，再核对 | 听说词汇必须"耳朵认识"，不只是"眼睛认识" |
| **造句输出法** | 每个情感词造1个与自己相关的句子并口头说出 | 如：I was nervous before the mid-term exam. |
| **发音三查** | 查重音（'grateful）、查尾音（excited的/d/）、查弱读连读 | 机评对发音准确敏感，拼对≠读对 |

### 六、高频易读错词汇自查表（广东考生专项）

| 词汇 | 常见错误 | 正确发音要点 |
|------|----------|--------------|
| grateful | 读成"great-full" | /ˈɡreɪtfl/，注意-ful弱化为/fl/ |
| excited | 尾音/d/吞掉 | /ɪkˈsaɪtɪd/，尾音/tɪd/完整 |
| disappointed | 重音错位 | /ˌdɪsəˈpɔɪntɪd/，重音在-poin- |
| museum | 重音错位 | /mjuˈziːəm/，重音在第二音节 |
| library | 读成"li-berry"吞音 | /ˈlaɪbrəri/，中间/brə/要清晰 |
| vegetables | 按拼写读成ve-ge-ta-bles | /ˈvedʒtəblz/，三音节 |
| comfortable | 按拼写读四音节 | /ˈkʌmftəbl/，口语三音节 |
| clothes | 读成"clo-thes"两音节 | /kləʊðz/，单音节 |
| usually | 读成"ju-zhu-a-lly" | /ˈjuːʒuəli/，/ʒ/音勿加卷舌 |
| world | /ɜː/音不到位 | /wɜːld/，区别于word /wɜːd/ |

## 备注

- 听说词汇的学习标准与笔试不同：**"四会"升级为"五会"**——会听、会读（发音准）、会说（脱口而出）、会写、会用。
- 使用建议：每天10个词，按"听音→跟读→造句→复述进小故事"四步走；每周用本卡词汇口头编一个1分钟小故事，直接对接Part C训练。
- 本卡词汇同时覆盖**广东中考→高考衔接**：多为初中已学词汇的"口语激活"，重点不是学新词，而是把认识的词练到"能说出来"。
- 情感词注意区分 **-ed / -ing**：excited（感到兴奋的，人作主语）vs. exciting（令人兴奋的，物作主语），复述时说错主语搭配会被扣语言分。
- 万能过渡句是"保底装备"：词汇量再大，没有过渡句串联，复述依然磕磕绊绊；先背熟过渡句框架，再往里面填词汇。
- 与笔试联动：本卡情感词、场景词同时是**读后续写和应用文写作**的基础素材，一卡两用；书面高级替换见写作相关卡片。
