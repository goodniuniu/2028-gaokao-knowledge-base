## 核心内容

> **本质理解**：句式升级不是"堆从句"，而是**信息重组**——把两个简单句合并为一个含非谓语/从句/倒装的复合句。每段有1-2个升级句即可，全篇5-8个高分句式是第五档的标配。

---

### 一、升级总原则："三变三不变"

| 原则 | 说明 |
|------|------|
| **变结构** | 简单句 → 从句/非谓语/倒装/强调 |
| **变开头** | 主语开头 → 状语/分词/with 开头 |
| **变连接** | and/but/so → while/whose/doing 等高级衔接 |
| **不变语义** | 升级后不能改变原意 |
| **不变正确** | 宁可写对的简单句，不写错的复杂句 |
| **不变节奏** | 长短句相间，全篇不要句句倒装 |

---

### 二、五大升级路径（基础句 → 升级句对照）

#### 路径1：定语从句升级（最稳妥）

| 基础句 | 升级句 | 技巧 |
|--------|--------|------|
| I received a letter. The letter was from my old friend. | I received a letter **which was from my old friend**. | which 合并 |
| The girl is my deskmate. Her father is a doctor. | The girl **whose father is a doctor** is my deskmate. | whose 表所属 |
| He said something. It surprised everyone. | He said something **that surprised everyone**. / **What he said** surprised everyone. | 双重升级选项 |
| She won first prize. It made her parents proud. | She won first prize, **which made her parents proud**. | which 指代整句（续写神句） |

> **续写应用**：She finally saw her son waiting at the gate, **which brought tears to her eyes**.

#### 路径2：非谓语开头升级（最有效）

| 基础句 | 升级句 | 技巧 |
|--------|--------|------|
| When he heard the news, he jumped with joy. | **Hearing the news**, he jumped with joy. | doing 表主动同时 |
| Because he was moved by her words, he couldn't say a word. | **Moved by her words**, he couldn't say a word. | done 表被动 |
| After he finished his homework, he went to bed. | **Having finished** his homework, he went to bed. | having done 表先后 |
| He walked into the room and held a bunch of flowers. | He walked into the room, **holding a bunch of flowers**. | doing 作伴随状语 |

> **口诀**："动作有先后，先动 having done；同时又主动，doing 往前搬；被动用 done，一句顶两番。"

#### 路径3：倒装与强调升级（最亮眼）

| 基础句 | 升级句 | 场景 |
|--------|--------|------|
| I realized the importance of family only then. | **Only then did I realize** the importance of family. | 续写结尾感悟（标配） |
| I had never felt so warm before. | **Never before had I felt** so warm. | 强烈情感 |
| He was so grateful that he hugged me tightly. | **So grateful was he that** he hugged me tightly. | so 提前倒装 |
| He not only helped me carry the books but also encouraged me. | **Not only did he help** me carry the books, **but he also encouraged** me. | 人物善举描写 |
| Her kindness changed my attitude. | **It was her kindness that** changed my attitude. | 强调句点题 |

#### 路径4：with 复合结构升级（最细腻，环境+神态描写）

| 基础句 | 升级句 | 场景 |
|--------|--------|------|
| She said "Thank you" and her eyes were full of tears. | "Thank you," she said, **with tears welling up in her eyes**. | 感动场景 |
| The boy stood there and his head was down. | The boy stood there, **with his head down**. | 羞愧/失落 |
| The wind was blowing gently and we walked along the beach. | **With the wind blowing gently**, we walked along the beach. | 环境烘托 |
| He left the room and the door was open. | He left the room **with the door open**. | 细节描写 |

> **续写万能神态模板**：
> - with + 身体部位 + 分词：**with her hands trembling**（手抖）/ **with his eyes shining**（眼亮）
> - with + 情感名词 + in/on 部位：**with a smile on her face** / **with regret in his heart**

#### 路径5：状语从句省略与高级衔接升级

| 基础句 | 升级句 | 技巧 |
|--------|--------|------|
| When he was asked about the secret, he kept silent. | **When asked** about the secret, he kept silent. | 状从省略主语+be |
| Although he was tired, he kept running. | **Tired as he was**, he kept running. | as 让步倒装 |
| He didn't know the truth, so he felt confused. | **Not knowing** the truth, he felt confused. | 否定分词 |
| If it is possible, I will visit you next week. | **If possible**, I will visit you next week. | if 省略（应用文常用） |

### 三、应用文专用高分句式（按功能分类）

| 功能 | 高分句式 | 例句 |
|------|----------|------|
| 开头点题 | I'm writing to + 目的 | I'm writing to invite you to the English speech contest. |
| 发出邀请 | We would be honored if... | We would be honored if you could be our judge. |
| 提出建议 | It is advisable/highly recommended that... | It is highly recommended that you (should) book tickets in advance. |
| 表达感谢 | I would appreciate it if... | I would appreciate it if you could reply at your earliest convenience. |
| 通知事项 | be scheduled to do / As scheduled | The contest **is scheduled to be held** in the school hall next Friday. |
| 说明意义 | not only...but also / which will surely... | The activity will not only enrich our school life but also broaden our horizons. |
| 结尾期待 | Looking forward to... / Your participation will surely... | Looking forward to your early reply. |

---

### 四、读后续写专用句式模块（按情节阶段分类）

| 阶段 | 句式模板 | 示例 |
|------|----------|------|
| **情绪爆发** | 情感名词 + seized/flooded over + sb. | A wave of relief **flooded over** her. |
| **动作链** | 动词1, 动词2, and 动词3 | He rushed to the door, opened it, and shouted her name. |
| **心理描写** | It suddenly dawned on sb. that... | It suddenly **dawned on me that** I had left the key at home. |
| **环境烘托** | 独立主格/with结构 | The rain pouring down, we quickened our pace. |
| **转折升华** | Only then/At that moment did sb. realize... | At that moment did I realize that love never fails. |
| **结尾点题** | It was + 核心词 + that... | It was a small act of kindness that lit up my whole day. |

### 五、避坑清单

- ❌ 为升级而升级：连续三句倒装/分词，读起来怪异——**每段最多2个升级句**，长短句相间。
- ❌ 分词开头逻辑主语错误：Walking on the road, a car nearly hit me. → 主句主语必须是"走"的发出者。
- ❌ with 结构写成 with sb. do：with + 宾语 + **doing/done/to do**，绝无动词原形。
- ❌ 应用文堆砌难词难句却偏离要点：应用文评分**要点齐全 > 语言华丽**，先覆盖全部要点再升级句式。
- ❌ 背诵模板张冠李戴：邀请信用了道歉信结尾——每个模板要连同"使用场景"一起记。
- ❌ 标点错误：which 指代整句时前面必须有逗号；Not only 倒装后 but also 分句用正常语序。

### 六、训练计划（6周句式内化）

| 周次 | 任务 | 检验标准 |
|------|------|----------|
| 第1周 | 背诵路径1、2的全部对照句，做10句"合并改写"练习 | 任意基础句30秒内给出升级句 |
| 第2周 | 背诵路径3倒装强调，仿写10句 | 倒装语序零错误 |
| 第3周 | 背诵路径4 with结构，写5个神态/环境描写句 | 每种with变体都会用 |
| 第4周 | 背诵应用文句式表，写1篇邀请信+1篇通知 | 要点齐全且含4个升级句 |
| 第5周 | 背诵读后续写句式模块，套写1篇真题风格续写 | 全文含6个高分句式且自然 |
| 第6周 | 限时写作实战（15分钟应用文+25分钟续写），自查句式多样性 | 形成个人"句式工具箱"（每类固定2个最顺手的模板） |

## 备注

- 使用顺序建议：**情节/要点先行，句式殿后**。写作时先确保读后续写情节合理、应用文要点齐全，再在誊写/修改阶段把3-5个简单句改写为升级句。
- 阅卷现场的真实情况：一个正确漂亮的倒装句或 with 结构，足以让阅卷老师对全篇印象提一档——但前提是全篇没有低级语法错误，先保正确再求高级。
- 建议建立个人"句式升级笔记本"：每次作文被老师画出的好句、范文中惊艳的句子，按本卡五大路径分类抄录，考前只翻这一本。
- 2023新高考Ⅰ卷读后续写"写作比赛"（巴西男孩Paul作文获奖）这类师生/成长主题，Only then did I realize... 与 with 神态结构均高度适配，可优先熟记这两个模板。
