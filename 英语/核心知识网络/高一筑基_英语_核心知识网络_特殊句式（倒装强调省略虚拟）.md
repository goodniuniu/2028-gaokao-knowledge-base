## 核心内容

> **本质理解**：特殊句式不是"花架子"——倒装与强调服务于**信息焦点的调整**，虚拟语气表达**与事实相反的假设**，省略追求**简洁**。写作中用一句倒装或强调，阅卷老师立刻识别出语言功底。

---

### 一、倒装（Inversion）

#### 1. 完全倒装（整个谓语提到主语前）

| 触发条件 | 例句 |
|----------|------|
| here, there, now, then 开头（主语是名词） | **Here comes** the bus.（但：Here **it comes**. 代词主语不倒装） |
| 方位副词/介词短语开头：up, down, in, out, away | **Out rushed** the children. |
| 表地点的介词短语置句首 | **In front of the house stands** an old tree. |

#### 2. 部分倒装（助动词/be/情态动词提到主语前）——高考主战场

| 触发词/结构 | 例句 | 备注 |
|-------------|------|------|
| **否定词开头**：never, seldom, rarely, hardly, little, nowhere, not until, not only, by no means | **Never have I seen** such a beautiful sunset. | 最常见考点 |
| **only + 状语**开头 | **Only then did I realize** my mistake. | 读后续写感悟句标配；only + 主语不倒装 |
| **so/such...that** 中 so/such 提前 | **So loudly did he speak** that everyone heard him. | 表"如此……以至于" |
| **hardly/scarcely...when, no sooner...than** | **No sooner had I arrived** than it began to rain. | 主句过去完成时+倒装 |
| **not only...but also** | **Not only did he win** the race, but he also broke the record. | 仅前半句倒装 |
| **as/though 让步倒装** | Tired **as** he was, he kept working. | 表语/状语提前，冠词省略 |
| **省略 if 的虚拟条件句** | **Were I** you, I would accept it. / **Had I known**, I would have helped. | 虚拟与倒装交叉考点 |

> **口诀**："否定开头要倒装，only状语也一样；so和such提到前，no sooner后had先。"

#### 3. 易错点

- only + 主语 开头**不倒装**：Only **Tom knows** the answer.
- 否定词在句中而非句首**不倒装**：I have never seen...（正常语序）
- as 让步倒装中名词提前**去冠词**：Child as he is, ...（≠ A child）

---

### 二、强调句型（It is/was ... that/who ...）

#### 1. 基本结构

```
It is/was + 被强调部分 + that/who + 句子其余部分
（强调人可用 who，其余一律 that）
```

| 强调成分 | 例句（原句：Tom bought a gift for his mother yesterday.） |
|----------|----------------------------------------------------------|
| 主语 | It was **Tom** that/who bought a gift for his mother yesterday. |
| 宾语 | It was **a gift** that Tom bought for his mother yesterday. |
| 时间状语 | It was **yesterday** that Tom bought a gift for his mother. |
| 目的状语 | It was **for his mother** that Tom bought a gift yesterday. |

#### 2. 三大变形与辨析

| 考点 | 例句 | 易错警示 |
|------|------|----------|
| 一般疑问式 | **Was it** yesterday **that** you met her? | 语序还原判断 |
| 特殊疑问式 | **When was it that** you met her? | 疑问词 + was it that |
| not...until 强调 | It was **not until midnight that** he came back. | that 后不再用倒装、不再否定 |

#### 3. 强调句 vs 定语从句 vs 主语从句（鉴别法）

> **去掉 It is/was...that，剩余部分仍能组成完整通顺的句子 → 强调句**；否则是定从或名从。

- It was in the park **that** I met her.（强调句：I met her in the park. ✔）
- It was the park **where** I met her.（定从：去掉后是 the park I met her，不成句 ✘）

#### 4. 谓语动词的强调（do/does/did + 动词原形）

- He **did come** last night.（他昨晚确实来了）
- Do be careful!（务必小心）

### 三、省略（Ellipsis）

| 类型 | 规则 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| **状语从句省略** | 当从句主语与主句主语一致（或从句主语为 it），且从句含 be 动词时，可省去"主语 + be" | When (**he was**) young, he loved reading. / If (**it is**) necessary, call me. |
| **不定式省略** | to 后重复的动词可省，保留 to | —Will you come? —I'd love **to**. |
| **比较从句省略** | 重复部分省略 | He runs faster than I (run). |
| **if so / if not / if any / if ever** | 固定省略结构 | There are few, **if any**, mistakes in his work. |
| **并列句省略** | 后句省略与前句相同的成分 | He is a teacher and his wife (is) a doctor. |

> **高考热点**：状语从句省略常与非谓语交叉考 —— When ______ (ask) about his plan, he kept silent. → **asked**（he was asked 的省略）。

---

### 四、虚拟语气（Subjunctive Mood）

#### 1. if 条件句三时态表格（必须默写）

| 假设时间 | if 从句 | 主句 | 例句 |
|----------|---------|------|------|
| **与现在相反** | did（be 用 were） | would/could/might + do | If I **were** you, I **would work** harder. |
| **与过去相反** | had done | would/could/might + have done | If you **had come** earlier, you **would have met** her. |
| **与将来相反** | did/were to do/should do | would/could/might + do | If it **should rain** tomorrow, we **would stay** home. |

> **错综时间**：从句与主句时间可不同 —— If you **had studied** hard before（过去）, you **would be** a top student now（现在）.

#### 2. 含蓄虚拟（无 if，但表虚拟）

| 信号词 | 例句 |
|--------|------|
| but for / without（要不是） | **Without** your help, I **would have failed**.（对过去） |
| otherwise / or（否则） | I was busy; **otherwise**, I **would have come**. |
| with + 名词 | **With** more time, we **could do** it better. |

#### 3. 特殊结构中的虚拟（高考语法填空热点）

| 结构 | 规则 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| **wish** + 宾从 | 现在：did/were；过去：had done；将来：would do | I wish I **could fly**. / I wish I **had taken** your advice. |
| **would rather** + 从句 | 现在/将来：did；过去：had done | I'd rather you **came** tomorrow. |
| **as if/though** | 与事实相反：did（现在）/ had done（过去） | He talks as if he **knew** everything. / He cried as if his heart **had broken**. |
| **It's (high) time that...** | did 或 should + do（should 不省） | It's high time we **went/should go** home. |
| **坚持/命令/建议/要求类动词 + that** | (should) + do，should 可省 —— 动词：suggest, advise, propose, demand, require, request, order, command, insist（坚持要求） | He suggested that we **(should) start** early. |
| 同类名词/形容词 + that | It is necessary/important/strange/natural that... (should) do | It is strange that he **(should) refuse** the offer. |

> **双义词陷阱**：suggest 表"表明"、insist 表"坚持认为（事实）"时**不用虚拟** —— His pale face **suggested** that he **was** ill. / He **insisted** that he **was** innocent.

### 五、写作加分句型速记（读后续写/应用文通用）

| 句型 | 模板 | 场景 |
|------|------|------|
| Only 倒装感悟句 | **Only then did I realize that** + 道理 | 续写结尾点题 |
| Not only 递进句 | **Not only did** sb. do..., **but** sb. also did... | 描述人物行为 |
| Never 倒装情感句 | **Never before had I felt** so + adj. | 强烈情感描写 |
| 强调句点题 | **It was** + 关键事物 + **that** changed everything. | 情节转折 |
| 虚拟遗憾句 | **If only** I had done...! | 悔恨、反思 |
| as 让步倒装 | + adj./adv. **as** sb. was/did, ... | 刻画人物品质 |

### 六、避坑清单

- ❌ Not only 后不倒装：Not only **he came**... → Not only **did he come**...
- ❌ Hardly...than / No sooner...when 张冠李戴：hardly/scarcely 配 **when**，no sooner 配 **than**。
- ❌ 强调句与 that 从句混用：It was at eight **when** he arrived. → 强调句必须用 **that**。
- ❌ wish 后时态不后移：I wish I **know**... → I wish I **knew**...
- ❌ 虚拟条件句主从句时态不配套：If I had known, I would tell you. → would **have told**。
- ❌ 状语从句省略后主语不一致：When reading, the light went out.（悬垂！）→ When **I was** reading, the light went out.

## 题型识别标志

> **看到什么条件 → 立刻想到什么方法/模型**

| 题干关键条件 | 识别为 | 首选方法 |
|-------------|--------|----------|
| 语法填空/改错句首见 never/seldom/only/not until | 部分倒装 | 助动词提到主语前 |
| 写作要求"高级句型"/"语言丰富" | 倒装/强调加分 | 用 Only then did I realize... / It was...that... |
| 从句 if I were you / Had I known | 虚拟/省略if倒装 | 还原为 if 条件句再判断时态 |
| 强调句空格在 It is/was ___ that | 强调结构 | 填 that/who（人） |
| 状语从句开头 When ___ (ask) | 省略（非谓语） | 填 asked（主语一致） |
| suggest/insist/demand 后 that 从句 | 虚拟 (should)+do | 注意双关（suggest表"表明"不虚拟） |
| 长难句拆解遇 "Only by..."/"Not only..." | 阅读倒装识别 | 还原正常语序理解句意 |

## 解题路径（特殊句式识别三步法）

> 特殊句式=信息焦点调整工具。三步锁定类型与形式，阅读拆解、写作升级、语法填空一鱼三吃。

### 第一步：抓标志词
- 倒装：never/seldom/only+状语/not only/hardly...when/as让步 → 部分倒装；here/there/方位词开头 → 完全倒装
- 强调：It is/was...that/who 框架
- 虚拟：if were/did/had done；wish/suggest类；without/but for
- 省略：when/if/while + 分词（主语一致）

### 第二步：判类型与还原
- 倒装→还原正常语序（助动词归位）再理解
- 虚拟→判断与何时相反（现在were/过去had done/将来should）
- 强调→删 It is/was...that，看是否成句（成句=强调句）
- 省略→补全省略的"主语+be"（如 When asked = When he was asked）

### 第三步：定形式、防坑
- 倒装：only+主语不倒装；否定词在句中不倒装
- 虚拟：主从句时态配套；suggest/insist 表事实不虚拟
- 强调：that 不可换 when/which；not...until 强调 that 后不倒装
- 省略：主语须与主句一致，否则悬垂错误

## 母题（2024 新课标Ⅰ卷·语法填空，15分）

> 广东考生真题（语数英使用新课标Ⅰ卷）。长难句拆解与特殊句式识别的实战母题。

**题目**（2024年新课标Ⅰ卷英语语法填空，节选）：
> Heatherwick Studio recently built a greenhouse at the edge of the National Trust's Woolbeding Gardens. This beautiful structure, named Glasshouse, is at the centre of a new garden that shows how the Silk Road influences English gardens even in modern times. ... The Glasshouse stands ____(63)____ a crowning achievement in contemporary design, to house the flora of sub-tropical south-west China at the end of a path retracing the steps along the Silk Route ____(64)____ brought the plants from their native habitat in Asia to come to define much of the ____(65)____ (rich) of gardening in England.

**答案**：63. as　64. that / which　65. richness

**解析**（三步法应用）：
- **第63题（介词）**：stand **as** a crowning achievement = "作为……而存在"，填介词 as。属"介词+名词"结构，非倒装。
- **第64题（定语从句·识别三步法）**：
  - 第一步抓标志：空格前是名词 the Silk Route，空格后突然出现的 brought 是谓语动词 → 必为定语从句缺主语。
  - 第二步判类型：先行词 the Silk Route（事物），在从句中作 brought 的主语，不可省略。
  - 第三步定形式：填关系代词 **that / which**。
- **第65题（词性转换）**：定冠词 the 后需名词，rich → **richness**（丰富性）。

> 💡 **关键技巧**：阅读长难句中"名词 + 空格 + 谓语动词"几乎必为定语从句/同位语。用三步法秒判关系词；写作中则主动调用倒装/强调（如 *Only then did I realize...* / *It was the Silk Road that...*）提升语言档——这正是广东卷读后续写的高分引擎。

**应用迁移（2023 新课标Ⅰ卷·读后续写真题句，写作加分示范）**：
> 真题材料节选（2023 新课标Ⅰ卷读后续写）：*When I handed in the essay to my teacher, he read it, laughed out loud, and said, "Great. Now, write it again."*
> 用特殊句式升级结尾点题（不改动原情节）：
> - 倒装感悟句：**Only then did I realize** that the joy of writing had nothing to do with winning.
> - 强调句点题：**It was my teacher's encouragement that** changed my attitude toward writing.
> - 虚拟遗憾句：**If only I had applied myself earlier**, I would have discovered the joy sooner.

## 备注

- 特殊句式在语法填空中直接考的空不多，但它"一鱼三吃"：阅读拆解长难句要用、写作升级要用、语法填空间接要考，投入产出比极高。
- 学习优先级：if 虚拟三时态表 > 部分倒装触发词 > 强调句鉴别 > 省略 > 完全倒装。
- 读后续写建议固定储备 2-3 个特殊句式模板（如 Only then did I realize... / Never had I felt...），考场直接调用，避免临时编造出错。
- "Were I you... / Had I known... / Should it rain..." 这类省略 if 的倒装虚拟，阅读中要能瞬间还原为 "If I were you..."，否则会误读为疑问句。
