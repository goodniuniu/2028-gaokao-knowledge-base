## 核心内容

### 题型识别标志
> **看到什么写作情境 → 立刻想到什么描写路径**

| 题干/原文情境 | 识别为 | 首选方法 |
|-------------|--------|----------|
| 原文有 **ran like crazy / jumped into / fished out** 等动词链 | 需延续动感风格 | 续写保持"具体动词+连贯顺序"，忌笼统 said/went |
| 段首语含 **ran back / called** 等动作 | 动作开头句 | 紧接该动作后的细节（如何跑、表情、下一步） |
| 需表现"急迫/慌乱" | 紧张动作群 | 用短句+急促动词（fumbled / dashed / breathless） |
| 高潮/助人场景 | 温暖动作 | 用轻柔精准动词（rest a hand / hand … over / pat） |

### 解题路径（动作描写三步法）

#### 第一步：拆解"动作链"
- 把一个笼统动作拆成 3-4 个连续微动作。劣质：*He gave me the money.* 优质：*He reached into his pocket, pulled out a few crumpled notes, and pressed them into my palm with a wink.*

#### 第二步：选"精准动词"替换弱动词
- 替换表：go→dash / make one's way；take→grab / seize；say→blurt / whisper；look→glance / stare / beam。
- 模板句式：**主语 + 精准动词 + 介词短语/方式状语**（突出怎么做的）

#### 第三步：动作+情感绑定
- 用动作外化情绪（见情感描写卡）：*He flashed me a big smile*（善意）而非 *He was kind*。

### 母题（2024 新课标Ⅰ卷·读后续写·Gunter，25分）

> 原文动作链（已核验）：*"I ran like crazy through the airport … jumped into the first taxi … Gunter fished out his little phone and rang up a friend … Gunter parked the taxi … turned around, and looked at me with a big smile."* 段首语1：*I ran back to Gunter and told him the bad news.*

**范文要点（动作描写示范）**：
- 第一段：*I spun around and sprinted back to the taxi. Gunter lowered the window; I blurted out the ATM failure, my voice trembling. Without a word, he dug into his coat, counted out a few bills, and tucked them into my hand. "Go—your bus is leaving!" he urged, waving me off.*
- 第二段：*I dialed his number the moment I landed. We met at a café; I slid the exact fare across the table and grasped his hand. "Thank you for trusting a stranger."*

> 💡 **关键技巧**：每段 2-3 条"精准动词链"；用 and / without a word / the moment 串联，制造画面节奏；避免 every sentence 以 He/She 开头。
