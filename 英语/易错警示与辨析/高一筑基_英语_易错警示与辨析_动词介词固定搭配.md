## 核心内容

> **本质理解**：介词搭配没有道理可讲（Why "depend on" not "depend at"?），只能靠"词块整体记忆"。最高效的方法：**按核心动词/核心形容词分组记，按介词线索归纳**。

### 一、六大核心动词短语动词表

#### 1. look 家族

| 短语 | 含义 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| look after | 照顾 | She looks after her little brother after school. |
| look for | 寻找 | What are you looking for? |
| look forward to | 期待（to为介词，接doing！） | I look forward to **hearing** from you. |
| look up | 查阅（字典）；抬头 | Look up the word in the dictionary. |
| look into | 调查 | The police are looking into the case. |
| look through | 浏览 | He looked through the notes before the exam. |

#### 2. turn 家族

| 短语 | 含义 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| turn on/off | 打开/关闭（电器） | Turn off the lights before leaving. |
| turn up/down | 调高/调低；出现/拒绝 | He didn't turn up at the meeting. / She turned down the offer. |
| turn out | 结果是（接to be/that从句） | It turned out to be a wonderful day. |
| turn into | 变成 | The old factory was turned into a museum. |
| turn to | 求助于；翻到 | You can always turn to your teachers for help. |

#### 3. get 家族

| 短语 | 含义 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| get on/along (well) with | 与……相处（融洽） | She gets along well with her classmates. |
| get over | 克服、恢复 | It took him months to get over the failure. |
| get used to | 习惯于（to为介词！） | You'll soon get used to **living** here. |
| get across | 使被理解 | He struggled to get his ideas across. |
| get rid of | 摆脱 | We should get rid of bad habits. |

#### 4. take 家族

| 短语 | 含义 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| take up | 占据（时间/空间）；开始从事 | Reading takes up much of my free time. / She took up painting last year. |
| take off | 起飞；脱下；走红 | The plane took off on time. |
| take over | 接管 | He took over the family business. |
| take in | 吸收；理解；欺骗 | I couldn't take in so much information at once. |
| take part in | 参加 | Over 200 students took part in the race. |

#### 5. put 家族

| 短语 | 含义 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| put off | 推迟（接doing！） | Never put off till tomorrow what you can do today. |
| put up | 张贴；搭建；留宿 | They put up a notice on the wall. |
| put up with | 忍受 | I can't put up with the noise any longer. |
| put forward | 提出（建议、计划） | He put forward a new proposal. |
| put out | 扑灭 | Firefighters put out the fire quickly. |

#### 6. break 家族

| 短语 | 含义 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| break down | 出故障；崩溃；分解 | The car broke down halfway. |
| break out | （战争、火灾、疫情）爆发 | A fire broke out in the building. |
| break into | 闯入 | Thieves broke into the house last night. |
| break up | 分手；解散；打碎 | The meeting broke up at noon. |

### 二、高频"动词+介词"固定搭配

| 搭配 | 含义 | 易错警示 |
|------|------|----------|
| depend **on** | 依赖、取决于 | ❌ depend of |
| insist **on** | 坚持（接doing） | insist on doing sth |
| succeed **in** | 成功（接doing） | ❌ succeed to do |
| apologize **to** sb **for** sth | 因某事向某人道歉 | 双介词结构 |
| congratulate sb **on** sth | 祝贺 | ❌ congratulate for |
| accuse sb **of** sth | 指控 | 与rob sb of / remind sb of / inform sb of / warn sb of同型 |
| prevent/stop/keep sb **from** doing | 阻止 | from可省（keep中不可省） |
| suffer **from** | 遭受（疾病、灾害） | ❌ suffer of |
| belong **to** | 属于 | 无被动、无进行时！ |
| focus **on** / concentrate **on** | 集中于 | |
| apply **for** | 申请 | apply to（适用于）勿混 |
| dream **of/about** | 梦想（接doing） | |
| object **to** | 反对（to为介词！） | object to doing sth |

### 三、高频"形容词+介词"固定搭配

| 介词 | 搭配 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| **of** | be afraid/proud/fond/tired/aware/ashamed **of** | We are proud of our school. |
| **for** | be famous/ready/sorry/grateful/responsible/eager **for** | She is grateful for your help. |
| **to** | be used/accustomed/close/similar/kind/polite/grateful **to** | This custom is new to me. |
| **with** | be angry/pleased/satisfied/popular/familiar/busy **with** | He is popular with students. |
| **in** | be interested/successful/rich/weak **in** | The country is rich in natural resources. |
| **at** | be good/bad/surprised/amazed/angry **at** | She is good at playing the piano. |
| **about** | be worried/crazy/curious/careful/serious **about** | Don't be worried about the result. |
| **from** | be different/absent/free **from** | My answer is different from yours. |

> **易错对比三连**：
> - be good **at** 擅长 / be good **for** 对……有益 / be good **to** 对……友善
> - be angry **with** sb 生某人的气 / be angry **at/about** sth 因某事生气
> - be familiar **with** sth 熟悉某物（人作主语）/ be familiar **to** sb 为某人所熟知（物作主语）

### 四、高频"名词+介词"固定搭配

| 搭配 | 含义 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| the key/answer **to** | ……的关键/答案 | Practice is the key to success. |
| access **to** | 接触/使用……的机会 | Students have access to the library. |
| the reason **for** | ……的原因 | What's the reason for your absence? |
| the effect/influence **on** | 对……的影响 | Music has a great effect on mood. |
| a lack **of** | 缺乏 | The project failed for a lack of funds. |
| an advantage **over** | 比……的优势 | Experience gives him an advantage over others. |
| contribution **to** | 对……的贡献 | Everyone should make a contribution to environmental protection. |
| attitude **to/towards** | 对……的态度 | a positive attitude to life |

> **黄金警示**：以上所有 **to** 都是**介词**，后面接名词或doing！the key to **solving** the problem；be used to **doing**。

### 五、"to是介词还是不定式"终极辨析（高考陷阱王）

| 类别 | 搭配 | 后面接 |
|------|------|--------|
| **介词to**（接doing） | look forward to / be used to / be accustomed to / object to / devote...to / pay attention to / stick to / lead to / get down to / prefer doing to doing | doing / 名词 |
| **不定式to**（接do） | want/hope/decide/manage/afford/refuse/promise/expect/choose to | 动词原形 |

> **判定口诀**："期待习惯反对，奉献注意坚持，导致开始宁愿——to后统统-ing。"

### 六、真题风格例句（语法填空考法还原）

1. The children are looking forward ______ visiting the science museum.（to）
2. He devoted his whole life ______ helping the poor.（to）
3. Fresh air is good ______ your health.（for）
4. The success of the project depends ______ everyone's effort.（on）
5. The old man was accused ______ stealing the bicycle.（of）
6. Regular exercise has a positive effect ______ our mood.（on）

## 备注

- 语法填空考介词时，先看空格**前**（动词/形容词/名词的固定搭配），再看空格**后**（名词短语），90%的介词空可用搭配直接锁定。
- "to是介词"类搭配是应用文写作的天然素材：I'm writing to express my gratitude... / I would appreciate it if... 写作与语法一举两得。
- 错词本只记"错的那半"：错了depend on就只抄depend **on**一个搭配，不要整页抄单词——针对性比数量重要。
- belong to无被动无进行时、look forward to后接doing，这两条是校级考试到高考通杀的陷阱，必须形成条件反射。
