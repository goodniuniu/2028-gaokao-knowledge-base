动词介词固定搭配（短语动词+形容词/名词+介词）

⚠️待强化★★★☆☆#高一筑基#新高考Ⅰ卷·广东来源：近5年新高考Ⅰ卷语法填空/完形填空高频搭配 + 课标必修一至三核心词块
广东考情：高频，近5年新高考Ⅰ卷语法填空几乎每年设1-2空考介词（多为固定搭配中的介词），完形填空常考短语动词语境辨析。难度定位为中档偏易，但遗忘率极高。新高考Ⅰ卷特色：介词空格前后往往隔几个词，考"长距离识别搭配"的能力；广东听说Part C复述时介词错误直接计入语言失分。
of：be afraid/proud/fond/tired/aware/ashamed **of** | We are proud of our school.
for：be famous/ready/sorry/grateful/responsible/eager **for** | She is grateful for your help.
to：be used/accustomed/close/similar/kind/polite/grateful **to** | This custom is new to me.
with：be angry/pleased/satisfied/popular/familiar/busy **with** | He is popular with students.
in：be interested/successful/rich/weak **in** | The country is rich in natural resources.
at：be good/bad/surprised/amazed/angry **at** | She is good at playing the piano.
about：be worried/crazy/curious/careful/serious **about** | Don't be worried about the result.
from：be different/absent/free **from** | My answer is different from yours.

核心内容

本质理解：介词搭配没有道理可讲（Why "depend on" not "depend at"?），只能靠"词块整体记忆"。最高效的方法：按核心动词/核心形容词分组记，按介词线索归纳。

一、六大核心动词短语动词表

1. look 家族

短语含义例句
look after照顾She looks after her little brother after school.
look for寻找What are you looking for?
look forward to期待（to为介词，接doing！）I look forward to hearing from you.
look up查阅（字典）；抬头Look up the word in the dictionary.
look into调查The police are looking into the case.
look through浏览He looked through the notes before the exam.

2. turn 家族

短语含义例句
turn on/off打开/关闭（电器）Turn off the lights before leaving.
turn up/down调高/调低；出现/拒绝He didn't turn up at the meeting. / She turned down the offer.
turn out结果是（接to be/that从句）It turned out to be a wonderful day.
turn into变成The old factory was turned into a museum.
turn to求助于；翻到You can always turn to your teachers for help.

3. get 家族

短语含义例句
get on/along (well) with与……相处（融洽）She gets along well with her classmates.
get over克服、恢复It took him months to get over the failure.
get used to习惯于（to为介词！）You'll soon get used to living here.
get across使被理解He struggled to get his ideas across.
get rid of摆脱We should get rid of bad habits.

4. take 家族

短语含义例句
take up占据（时间/空间）；开始从事Reading takes up much of my free time. / She took up painting last year.
take off起飞；脱下；走红The plane took off on time.
take over接管He took over the family business.
take in吸收；理解；欺骗I couldn't take in so much information at once.
take part in参加Over 200 students took part in the race.

5. put 家族

短语含义例句
put off推迟（接doing！）Never put off till tomorrow what you can do today.
put up张贴；搭建；留宿They put up a notice on the wall.
put up with忍受I can't put up with the noise any longer.
put forward提出（建议、计划）He put forward a new proposal.
put out扑灭Firefighters put out the fire quickly.

6. break 家族

短语含义例句
break down出故障；崩溃；分解The car broke down halfway.
break out（战争、火灾、疫情）爆发A fire broke out in the building.
break into闯入Thieves broke into the house last night.
break up分手；解散；打碎The meeting broke up at noon.

二、高频"动词+介词"固定搭配

搭配含义易错警示
depend on依赖、取决于❌ depend of
insist on坚持（接doing）insist on doing sth
succeed in成功（接doing）❌ succeed to do
apologize to sb for sth因某事向某人道歉双介词结构
congratulate sb on sth祝贺❌ congratulate for
accuse sb of sth指控与rob sb of / remind sb of / inform sb of / warn sb of同型
prevent/stop/keep sb from doing阻止from可省（keep中不可省）
suffer from遭受（疾病、灾害）❌ suffer of
belong to属于无被动、无进行时！
focus on / concentrate on集中于
apply for申请apply to（适用于）勿混
dream of/about梦想（接doing）
object to反对（to为介词！）object to doing sth

三、高频"形容词+介词"固定搭配

介词搭配例句
ofbe afraid/proud/fond/tired/aware/ashamed ofWe are proud of our school.
forbe famous/ready/sorry/grateful/responsible/eager forShe is grateful for your help.
tobe used/accustomed/close/similar/kind/polite/grateful toThis custom is new to me.
withbe angry/pleased/satisfied/popular/familiar/busy withHe is popular with students.
inbe interested/successful/rich/weak inThe country is rich in natural resources.
atbe good/bad/surprised/amazed/angry atShe is good at playing the piano.
aboutbe worried/crazy/curious/careful/serious aboutDon't be worried about the result.
frombe different/absent/free fromMy answer is different from yours.

易错对比三连：
- be good at 擅长 / be good for 对……有益 / be good to 对……友善
- be angry with sb 生某人的气 / be angry at/about sth 因某事生气
- be familiar with sth 熟悉某物（人作主语）/ be familiar to sb 为某人所熟知（物作主语）

四、高频"名词+介词"固定搭配

搭配含义例句
the key/answer to……的关键/答案Practice is the key to success.
access to接触/使用……的机会Students have access to the library.
the reason for……的原因What's the reason for your absence?
the effect/influence on对……的影响Music has a great effect on mood.
a lack of缺乏The project failed for a lack of funds.
an advantage over比……的优势Experience gives him an advantage over others.
contribution to对……的贡献Everyone should make a contribution to environmental protection.
attitude to/towards对……的态度a positive attitude to life

黄金警示：以上所有 to 都是介词，后面接名词或doing！the key to solving the problem；be used to doing。

五、"to是介词还是不定式"终极辨析（高考陷阱王）

类别搭配后面接
介词to（接doing）look forward to / be used to / be accustomed to / object to / devote...to / pay attention to / stick to / lead to / get down to / prefer doing to doingdoing / 名词
不定式to（接do）want/hope/decide/manage/afford/refuse/promise/expect/choose to动词原形

判定口诀："期待习惯反对，奉献注意坚持，导致开始宁愿——to后统统-ing。"

六、真题风格例句（语法填空考法还原）

  1. The children are looking forward ______ visiting the science museum.（to）
  2. He devoted his whole life ______ helping the poor.（to）
  3. Fresh air is good ______ your health.（for）
  4. The success of the project depends ______ everyone's effort.（on）
  5. The old man was accused ______ stealing the bicycle.（of）
  6. Regular exercise has a positive effect ______ our mood.（on）

七、训练计划（3周滚动）

周次任务检测方式
第1周每天背1个动词家族（6天）+ 周日默写全部例句汉译英默写
第2周动词+介词、形容词+介词各3天，周日综合语法填空风格填空20题
第3周"to是介词"专项 + 名词+介词，错词本整理旧错重测，正确率需≥90%

备注

  • 语法填空考介词时，先看空格前（动词/形容词/名词的固定搭配），再看空格后（名词短语），90%的介词空可用搭配直接锁定。
  • "to是介词"类搭配是应用文写作的天然素材：I'm writing to express my gratitude... / I would appreciate it if... 写作与语法一举两得。
  • 错词本只记"错的那半"：错了depend on就只抄depend on一个搭配，不要整页抄单词——针对性比数量重要。
  • belong to无被动无进行时、look forward to后接doing，这两条是校级考试到高考通杀的陷阱，必须形成条件反射。
