## 核心内容

> **本质理解**：易混动词的错误根源有三——①词形相近（lie/lay）；②中文释义相同（"借"borrow/lend）；③及物/不及物不分（rise/raise）。辨析的核心方法是：**先判及物性，再判方向，最后记搭配**。

---

### 一、词形相近类：lie / lay（高考第一大坑）

#### 三组形式必须背熟

| 动词 | 含义 | 过去式 | 过去分词 | 现在分词 | 及物性 |
|------|------|--------|----------|----------|--------|
| lie | 躺、位于 | **lay** | **lain** | lying | 不及物（不接宾语） |
| lie | 说谎 | lied | lied | lying | 不及物 |
| lay | 放置、下蛋 | **laid** | **laid** | laying | **及物**（必须接宾语） |

> **记忆口诀**："躺"的过去式是lay（躺下→lay），"说谎"加-ed（lied）很规则，"放置/下蛋"有a（laid）又及物。

#### 真题风格例句

- He **lay** on the grass, staring at the stars.（躺的过去式，勿写成lied）
- The hen has **laid** three eggs this week.（下蛋，及物+现在完成时）
- Don't believe him — he **lied** about his age.（说谎，规则变化）
- The town **lies** in a valley between two mountains.（位于）

#### 避坑清单
- ❌ He lied down for a rest. → ✅ He **lay** down for a rest.
- ❌ laying on the beach → ✅ **lying** on the beach（躺无宾语用lie）

---

### 二、词形相近类：rise / raise / arise

| 动词 | 含义 | 过去式/过去分词 | 及物性 | 典型搭配 |
|------|------|----------------|--------|----------|
| rise | 上升、升起、起身 | rose / risen | 不及物 | The sun rises. / Prices rose. |
| raise | 举起、提高、抚养、筹集 | raised / raised（规则） | **及物** | raise your hand / raise money / raise children |
| arise | 出现、产生（问题、困难） | arose / arisen | 不及物 | A problem arose. |

> **记忆口诀**：rise自然升（太阳、物价自己涨），raise人为举（举手、筹钱要宾语），arise问题起（抽象事物出现）。

#### 真题风格例句
- Temperatures are expected to **rise** sharply this week.（自然上升，不及物）
- The charity **raised** over 10,000 yuan for the flood victims.（筹集，及物）
- Should any difficulty **arise**, please contact us immediately.（正式文体"若出现困难"）

---

### 三、中文同义类：say / speak / tell / talk

| 动词 | 核心用法 | 固定搭配 | 易错警示 |
|------|----------|----------|----------|
| say | 强调**说的内容** | say sth (to sb) | ❌ say sb sth（✅ say sth to sb） |
| speak | 强调**说的动作/能力/语言** | speak English; speak to sb; speak at the meeting | "说某种语言"只用speak |
| tell | 强调**告诉、讲述** | tell sb sth; tell a story/lie/the truth; tell sb to do | 可双宾语：tell me the truth |
| talk | 强调**交谈、聊天** | talk to/with sb; talk about sth | ❌ talk sth（✅ talk about sth） |

#### 真题风格例句
- "The exam is next Friday," she **said** to the class.（内容）
- The professor was invited to **speak** at the opening ceremony.（发言）
- Grandma used to **tell** us stories about her childhood.（讲故事）
- I need to **talk** to you **about** the summer camp plan.（交谈+话题）

> **口诀**：say内容、speak语言、tell告诉可双宾、talk交谈要about。

---

### 四、中文同义类：borrow / lend / keep

| 动词 | 方向 | 搭配 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|------|
| borrow | **借入** | borrow sth **from** sb | I borrowed a dictionary from the library. |
| lend | **借出** | lend sth **to** sb / lend sb sth | Could you lend me your pen? |
| keep | **借用多久**（延续性） | keep sth for + 时间段 | You can keep the book for two weeks. |

> **高考陷阱**：How long can I **keep** the book?（勿用borrow/lend——瞬间动词不与时间段连用）

---

### 五、其他高频六组对比表

| 词组 | 辨析要点 | 例句 |
|------|----------|------|
| **win / beat** | win + 比赛/奖项/战争；beat + 对手（人/队） | We **won** the game. / We **beat** them 3-0. |
| **cost / spend / take / pay** | cost主语是物（sth costs sb money）；spend主语是人（spend...on/(in) doing）；take用it作形式主语（It takes sb time to do）；pay主语是人+for（pay money for sth） | The trip **cost** us 2,000 yuan. / She **spent** two hours on homework. / It **took** me a week to finish the novel. / He **paid** 50 yuan for the ticket. |
| **sit / seat** | sit不及物（sit down）；seat及物（seat oneself / be seated） | Please **be seated**. / The hall **seats** 500 people.（容纳） |
| **happen / take place** | happen偶然发生；take place有计划地发生 | An accident **happened** last night. / The sports meeting will **take place** next Monday. |
| **join / join in / take part in** | join + 组织/人群（join the army/us）；join in/take part in + 活动 | She **joined** the music club. / Everyone **took part in** the cleanup. |
| **listen / hear** | listen (to) 强调动作；hear强调结果 | I **listened** carefully but **heard** nothing. |

### 六、易混动词速查口诀

> 躺lay下，放置laid；rise自己升，raise要宾语。
> say说内容，tell可双宾；borrow借进from，lend借出to。
> win赢比赛，beat赢对手；物cost人spend，it用take，付款pay for。
> happen是偶然，take place有计划；瞬间动作别接how long，延续要用keep。

### 七、语境辨析练习（高考风格）

在下列句子中选择正确动词（答案附后）：

1. The price of vegetables has ______ (risen/raised) by 20% this month.
2. She ______ (lay/laid) the baby gently on the bed.
3. How much did you ______ (spend/pay) for the concert ticket?
4. Our team ______ (won/beat) the championship for the third time.
5. Could you ______ (say/tell) me the way to the museum?
6. The ceremony will ______ (happen/take place) as scheduled.

> **答案**：1.risen 2.laid 3.pay 4.won 5.tell 6.take place
